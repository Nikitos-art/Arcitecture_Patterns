Bio and Education

I was born in Pavlovskaya Sloboda. It's a small suburban town right nearby Moscow and lived there untill I was 15. That's when my mom and my future (at that time) stepfather when to the USA. We lived right near Houston, Texas. I went to a local high school there. I was a sophmore and was in my high school soccer team. I scored 4 goals in that season! And they even announced my name on PA system a couple of times. Unfortunately we couldn't keep living in the USA, so we decided to travel and go to Shanghai, China. That's when my story begins. Life in China was a whole life chapter for me. When I turned 18 I wento to different countryside schools in China and taught English there. Mostly in Zhejiang and Fujian provinces. I was also on provincial TV there a few times when I had to speak Chinese. It was fun! After 10 years in China my life took another turn and I had to go back to Russia. It was to go back and I didn't really feel home. After the wasr in Ukraine, I moved to neighboring Georgia. Now my family and I are making plans to get to the USA for good since my mom is a naturalized citizen there. I love my parents and really want to see them there.
